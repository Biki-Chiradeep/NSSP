Pollution Free Nation Foundation

(PFNF)

About Organization

Keeping an eye on the Climate change and Global warming, POLLUTION FREE NATION has been working on different projects since 2009. Our prime focus is to maintain a better environment for our next generation. We are committed towards making a ZERO CARBON NATION. It is indeed a difficult tusk and efforts can be given to the fullest.

Prime focus

Extensive Campaign from Grass Root Level to the top, Awareness programs and Campaign in all Districts of the nation, District level monitoring and competitions, Special training programme in Hospitality Industry and Health Care segment, Involving maximum number of citizen in the campaign, Special programs for Cleanliness, Door to door Campaign, Monitoring Pollution level and Oxygen level on a daily basis, Awareness programs to make people understand the effect of different Plantations.
